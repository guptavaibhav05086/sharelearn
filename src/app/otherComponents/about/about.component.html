About Us

mLearn - Unbundle Your learning

Learn with your own pace

We are the First organization which understood the importance of micro learning. With our decade long experience with Technical learning domain we realized that most of the candidates can easily understand and learn the basics of any technology but 90% of them face issues when they try to dig deeper in to the complex topics and without the proper guidance and lack on hands on experience they are not able to master them and gain the confidence in that particular technology stack.

All the course providers in the market provides the entire bundled packages of the course with all those topics which a candidate already knows and ends up paying even for those which he already knows. We in mLearning understood that gap in the industry and came up with a solution of Pay as you learn on which a candidate only needs to pay for the topics which he/she wants to learn instead of paying for the entire bundled package. Also if someone wants to learn from basics till advance they can choose our bundled package course in which also they choose pay as you learn approach where candidate can choose to pay as per the course progress instead of paying everything in one go and have an option to choose among multiple trainers in they are not satisfied with the existing one. All our course are instructor led programs. Candidate can choose for online as well as the classroom base session. In the classroom session candidate need to visit the designated center to attend the session.

Learning Options

On our platform you can choose any of the below mentioned learning Plans

Micro Learning (Pay as Learn):

Under this program candidate can chose to pay only for the topics which he wants to learn.

  1. Once candidate registers for a particular topic he/she will be mapped with the relevant trainers from the pool of our experienced trainers.
  2. You can shortlist anyone of them based of the different parameters and you will get a chance to interact with them before starting with your learning.
  3. Once candidate is satisfied he/she need to make the payment though the portal.
  4. After that you will receive the meeting link along with other relevant details regarding your session.
