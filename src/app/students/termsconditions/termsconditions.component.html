Order will be assigned only to the designer who first accepts the order.

 

  1. Designer shall be required to immediately report the User/Company of any illegal/inappropriate content received to him and should not proceed further for designing.

Designer must join the meeting through google meet on the allotted time slot and should share his screen for live designing/correction/revision.

 

  1. Only registered designer shall be able to participate in a meeting with the User.

  4. Both the User and Designer shall be bound by the timings as fixed for the meeting but in case of the unavailability or delay by the User, the designer will have to wait for 40 minutes.
  5. If the designer didn’t turn up on the meeting at the specified time, then the user will wait for 15 minutes, and can leave as per his convenience after the expiry of the said time if the designer didn’t join the meeting and accordingly the designer will be termed as not available.
