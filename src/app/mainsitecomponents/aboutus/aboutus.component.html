Who we are- About shapeNprint.

shapeNprint is an Indian freelancing platform for graphic designers and printing delivery startup founded by Gaurav Agrawal and Savan Agrawal in 2020. Its main branch is in Bangalore, Karnataka.

"We believe in creating an innovative circle of graphic designers and printers who contribute to customer solutions and showcase their knack of rejuvenating and vivifying ideas and emotions."
