4 Secret Tips to Design a Perfect Fabric Banner

Banners are generally big size cloth or non-cloth advertising and promotion tools that are majorly used to attract leads through one-way communication. Fabric banners are pieces of clothes bearing the promotional message or company's name, logo, signs, and signals.

They are a simple and effective means of marketing. They are presented as extensive advertising tools.

Here go to five essential things to learn before making a fabric banner. . 

1) Banners are usually made of cloth


Banners can be made of several other materials. These banners vary in thickness, pliability and durability. Their consistency is not used as a parameter to differentiate or choose the fabric.

But more importantly, they are measured based on weight. We find cloth banners that are 9oz or 250gsm material. This indicates the value of the fabric.  

2) Fabric for Banners

Fabric banners are usually made of polyester or satin. They offer colourful graphics. Some advantages of using them include that they are machine washable and crease-resistant. They are recommended to be displayed indoors. However, grand openings, sports events, Trade shows, etc. can make use of banners.

Keeping outdoors can be detrimental to its quality. Fabric banner uses lightweight, 9 oz. Polyester.

3) Fabric banner sizes

Although there is no standard sizing of banners, there are a few sizes that are commonly used. As the use of cloth banner is quite vivid, sizing can better be determined using previous orders and history of banner delivery.

4) Why can fabric banners be preferred?

Dye-sublimation printing is used for printing fabric banners. This makes the banners more attractive, affects their looks positively, makes it look colourful and vibrant.

Using vinyl banner can impact the perception of clients negatively as they look inferior in quality. Choosing the right fabric is essential, so banners are durable, rich in visual quality and attractive.
