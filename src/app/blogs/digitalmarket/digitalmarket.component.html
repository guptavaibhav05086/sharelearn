How to Create Perfect Digital Marketing Banner Ads?

Social media banner is a graphical representation of a brand and presents its serviceability. Social media banners take care of social profiles and draw in traffic through beautiful designs and graphics.

Digital advertising banners have become an important advertising tool in today’s world, where people tend to spend most of their time on social media and webspace.

This is known as Banner Advertising. It is defined as the approach of using images and pictorial shows/exhibits to pull in visitors or for promotional purposes. The horizontal type advertisement banner is called a leaderboard, and the vertical banners are called a skyscraper, positioned on a web page’s sidebars.

Here are five things to keep in mind while designing a digital banner which never fails to call for actions. 

1) Basic information banners should present

The company logo needs to be there for brand promotion and brand recognition. You are flaunting the company logo to ensure your widespread dissemination of brand information.

Catching attention through attractive phrases and offers which beckons is a must. Going through a thousand ads every day, what makes your advertisement unique and special among others is what needs to be focused and deliberated upon.

And lastly mentioning the call for taking action as to fulfil the purpose of advertising to create traffic is the ultimate intention.  

2) Should look elegant and dominant among others

In terms of image requirement, it depends if you want to put an image which directly relates to what you want to present and promote or not.

Right graphics and typography are essential.

Choosing appropriate Colors is very important in digital banners for attracting users to take a look at the advertisement. Different colours represent different emotions, so researching what’s best is imperative.

Animations can be used as pop up to induce the user to look at the banner. Something moving catches more attention than something still. 

3) The text makes everything possible

Minimum words should use on the banner, text powerful enough to make people read further and take action.

Using cursive fonts, Majuscule copy or font sizes smaller than a 10 pt unless stated otherwise.

4) Some important banner requirements

Banner size - there are a lot of sizes available for ads, but some sizes are popular and poised to have got more clicks than others. So searching for banners of the right size is significant.

Banner ad file size should be as small as it can be to load quickly than others as the user moves through the host page even if it takes time to charge the page entirely.

A correct file format should be used. Html5, png, jpg, gif are used commonly for digital banners.
