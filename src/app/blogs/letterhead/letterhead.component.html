Why are Letterheads Important for Your Business Letters?

Letterheads are the blank imprinted sheets of papers designed and personalised for a company with a company logo, address and professional information on top, which serves to send greetings or formal introduction to various prospects and leads.

Letterheads can be a whole way of branding. It's not often that you receive a letterhead mail from an organisation. It's a pre-printed heading on letters, memos, notes. Letterhead is essential for consolidating the company's position to help look more professional and to promote the company's brand image.

Here are a few points needed to remember while creating a new letterhead for your business. 

1) Size of letterheads


The size of letterheads is crucial as it's standard and can not be customised. Letterheads are printed on A4 size, so they should be designed accordingly.

The Letterhead style should effectively draw the reader's attention to the company name, so making the company name appear in the broadest form is exceptionally crucial. The other items’ font size should be between 10 and 12 points, so the heading doesn't seem crowded.

 

2) Letterhead comprises:
  • The company or individual is responsible
  • A return address
  • Contact number or Email address

Nothing else needs to be mentioned in a letterhead. Unsolicited messages or information will only make a Letterhead look unpleasant.

3) The perfect hierarchy

The hierarchy of information should be maintained in a letterhead. The more valuable information should be made more accessible and transparent. The place can determine the significance of the data it is being awarded in the Letterhead. Legibility and attractive fonts will help in focussing more on using the Letterhead appropriately.

4) Alignment makes it's unique

An incredible way to make your Letterhead distinct and famous is by using better and uncommon margins around your Letterhead. Taking care of margins, line alignment, and text positioning is required.

Using elegant and sober colour schemes pertinent to the brand logo will be helpful. 

5) Letterheads can also turn heads

You can make your Letterhead visually enticing by making use of spot UV design overprint.

Letterheads consist of imprinted lines to use them conveniently to write without worrying about alignment and bending of the supposedly straight line.

Adding 'quotes and sayings' in the Letterhead can encourage people to work diligently and energetically.
