Why the Design of a Roll-Up Standee Matters for Advertising?

The standee banners are an essential part of marketing tools to let prospects know about what you do and showcase your established brand. These roll-up standees placed at selected easily sighted locations can facilitate a brand position and brand promotion for your company.

A roll-up standee does a lot from saving high costs of creation to saving delivery costs. It is convenient to be used as an advertising tool that you would find it everywhere in events.

Roll up standees are self-standing and the most common tools for imparting information and displaying highlights of an event, seminar or fest. They are light and attract bystanders' attention and are used for publicity.

Here are five reasons why roll up standees are the best. . 

1) They are light and convenient to carry


The roll-up standees are relatively light and sizeable. They are not huge and can easily carry around. They are portable and available to be moved around at any point in time. It's better to use roll-up standees if convenience and portability are the significant factors involved. 

2) The roll-up standees are visible to any bystander

If placed at appropriate locations from where they are visible to anybody, roll up standees can help brand promotion. Roll-up standees are used explicitly as a pop-up banner stands. They catch the bystander's eye more easily than giant hanging banners or other promotional tools.

For all sorts of heights, they are the most useful and likely to be witnessed by onlookers.

A person can skip looking at the giant hanging advertising board, but moving on without paying attention to a standee is unlikely to keep the right location and position in mind.

3) Cost-effective and saves promotional costs

Compared with other advertising tools, the roll-up standees are less expensive, considering their onlookers’ impact.

It not only saves costs for setting it up but also saves replacement costs as they can be used for the long term.

4) Durability and reusability

The roll-up standees are incredibly durable and useful with the material intact for a longer duration.

They are not easy to be affected by natural extremities, and the material it stands on is also long-lasting, making the print and the roll-up standees more durable.

Their durability can be easily linked to their reusability as they can be stored and used again whenever required.  

5) The material used for standees

Polypropylene is majorly used in the manufacture of stands.

For creating banners, star media flex and digital vinyl are used.

Aluminium frames are used for the roll-up standees. They come with aluminium rods, stands, etc.  

6) Weight and printing of the standees

Standees are easy to carry and can move as and when required.

Their weight is just as maximum as between 2.5 and 3 kg.

Digital printing is used for printing on roll-up standees. Digital printing refers to the method of printing a digital-based image directly. Digital sources are printed with the help of high-volume laser or inkjet printers.
