How to Design a Perfect Promotional Coupon?

Promotional coupons are a way to advertise and lure customers into furthering their interest in the brand. They are tangible and intangible and can be received and realized at the time of any purchase activity or registering action.

A promotional coupon is a promotional tool used in the form of a digital or physical document. They can be redeemed during the time of a transaction for receiving or allowing a discount. Coupons are dispensed through electronic mails and other applications.

Coupons can be of different types ranging from discount types, free giveaways to buy-one-get-one and free trial offers.

Make a coupon in just four steps. Here's the guide. 

1) Use personalized and original images to promote the brand


Graphics accentuating the brand, the logo, the offer available, promo codes, and where it can be redeemed, and the conditions must be used. Captivating icons and Graphics might be used for coupons designing.

 

2) Fonts and colours should be simple

Fonts used for coupons Should be legible, simple, and bold. They can contribute a lot in grabbing attention.

Font size and colour schemes should be chosen according to or contrasting to the brand logo highlighting the brand.

3) Mentioning the expiry date is essential

People guesstimate considering the expiry date of their future course of action if they want to accept it. Expiry dates on coupons are critical as time duration for redemption is always limited, and it urges customers to take quick actions and decisions.

4) Stimulating interest

Creating a buzz among prospective buyers through coupons is an excellent means of promotion. Coupons can be used to draw in people to become regular and permanent customers. Coupons can sometimes encourage people to try and find about your brand. Interest can be generated in several ways by using graphics and icons well aligned with customer needs.

Though a coupon is not supposed to look like fancy banners, it should be as attractive to catch enough attention so that people give a glance to decide if it is worth their time or not. Pop-ups should be used to grab attention. Shapes, format and sizes can be determined accordingly to allure people.
